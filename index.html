How does it all start, how can it later affect us?

Beauty standards have started ever since early slavery, but it mainly started in today's socitey with social media. With social media growing, we get a daily reminder of how we aren't beautiful. When we see our favorite influencers posting, it's another reminder that we don't fit with those standards. Studies have shown such ideas can possiby lead to depression, reduce of self-esteem, eating disorders and much more.
